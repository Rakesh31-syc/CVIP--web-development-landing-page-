EdTech online education

GEducational Technology, commonly referred to as EdTech, is the integration of technology into education to enhance and support learning and teaching processes. It encompasses a wide array of tools, platforms, and methods that leverage technological advancements to improve the educational experience. In recent years, EdTech has gained significant traction due to its potential to address various challenges in education and transform learning into a more engaging, accessible, and effective endeavor.
